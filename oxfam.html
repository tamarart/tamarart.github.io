Intermón Oxfam

Web design for a better world.

Designed at http Comunicació. Barcelona 2012
Skills: Visual Design, Art Direction.
With Joana Filizola & Álvaro García

About the project

Oxfam is a group of organisations working around the world to find solutions to poverty and injustice. The goal with Oxfam was to build a corporative website based on a CMS including all the usefull information for the users.

My role

I worked as part of a team beside of the UX designer. I transformed the wireframes and information architecture into visual mockups. My duty was to create brand assets and built and maintain a visual style guide for the development team, according with the specs of the platform. We did also many iterations to get the content and layouts looking perfect.
