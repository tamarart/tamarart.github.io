About ROOOF

The goal of ROOOF is to allow users to discover and share unique events performed in privat houses around Barcelona on a social platform. I worked as part of a team in our final Master's degree project. We started from zero, interviewing users and focusing on finding the best solution for a giving initial brief.

View the wireframes prototype

The process

Before starting anything, we spent time discussing the vision of the platform through a design thinking process. We wireframed and made low profile prototypes using marvel app, which we tested with users. We used sass, foundation and git to craft the code, and built a live style guide to help us to maintain a solid and reusable style in every element, following the approach of atomic design.

View the Style Guide

The platform

The final result is a hand made fully responsive prototype, working in an agile process and applying desing thinking methodologies.
